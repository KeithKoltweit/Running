Helpful Guide to Start Running

Running is a difficult hobby to get into. We want to make a place for runners just starting out with basic information that will get you to the next level. Wether thats training for your first 5K all the way up to a Full Marathon. Are main focus is to give you accurate information and keep you motivated.

Tips to start out:

  • Write down your goal
  • Consistent running time
  • Proper running gear
  • Write down your results
  • Proper nutrition

Methods to consider:

  • Find a running buddy
  • Find a running program
  • Focus on technique
  • Explore new paths
  • Word of encouragement

Tools to stay Inspired:

  • Music
  • Gear
  • Smart Watch
  • Training Log
  • Training Coach

Let's get out and get some miles done!
