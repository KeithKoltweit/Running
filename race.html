Upcoming Races

If you've made it to this page, good for you! Is it time to put your training to the test? A great way to begin racing is to start off small agian, we recommend beginning with a 5K. We have a variety of race distances that you can try out. A friendly reminder, don't buy a brand new pair of shoes for your race. We recommend a broken in pair of shoes to minimize blistering.

Helpful tips for first time racers.

  • Arrive early
  • Bring a post run snack
  • Set a realistic time to finish
  • Stretch befor and after run
  • Enjoy your time
