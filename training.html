Training Schedule

We have set up a couple tables to get you started with your documentation. Feel free to create your own. In the beginning, many new runners stumble. You start out with the best intentions - but a few minutes later you want to quit. Just remember this takes time. Don't be afraid to utilize a walk/run method.If distance is not your pace yet, set a time and just make it to that point. You'll be suprised at how fast you are going to progress everyday!
